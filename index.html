About Me

Hello! I’m a web development intern learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, passionate about creating visually appealing and user-friendly websites.

My journey into tech started with curiosity and grew into a deep interest in building things for the web. I’m especially drawn to front-end development and UI/UX design.

Beyond coding, I enjoy exploring new tech tools, participating in coding challenges, and contributing to open-source projects. I believe in continuous learning and love experimenting with new frameworks and libraries.

When I’m not coding, you’ll find me reading blogs, watching tech videos, sketching wireframes, or planning my next side project. I’m also passionate about photography and love capturing aesthetic moments.

My ultimate goal is to become a skilled full-stack developer and build applications that make people’s lives easier and more connected.
